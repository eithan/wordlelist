First and Last Letters

S is king of the opening slot — 14.8% of all answers start with it, ahead of C (8.7%) and B (7.5%). No answer has ever started with X, and only two started with Z (ZEBRA, ZESTY).

E dominates the final slot at 19.8%, followed by Y (13.9%) — surprisingly strong for a letter that's weak overall — then T (11.3%) and R (8.5%).

The famous plural trap: only 1.5% of answers end in S, and simple plurals essentially never appear. If you're about to guess one, don't.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most common letter in Wordle answers?

E — it appears in 47% of all real answers, followed by A (40%) and R (36%). Nearly one in five answers ends in E.

How often do Wordle answers have repeated letters?

About 31% of all answers contain at least one repeated letter. LL, OO and EE are the most common doubles — see the full breakdown above.

Has a Wordle answer ever had no vowels?

Six times: CRYPT, GLYPH, MYRRH, NYMPH, SHYLY and TRYST — all carried by Y.

Do Wordle answers ever end in S?

Only about 1.5% do, and simple plurals essentially never appear. It's one of the most common traps — read more in our strategy guide.
